instructions

  1. In the beginning of the game you will be given 10 chances to roll the dice
  2. Each time you roll the same number you will receive 100 points
  3. If your 10 chances has been used you can add another 8 chances
  4. You can choose if you want to add chances or not
  5. 80 points will be deducted each time you add chances
  6. You cannot add chances if your points is lower than 80 points
  7. The limit point of this game is 600 points
  8. If you reach the limit points you won else you lose
  9. You can always reset or quit the game whenever you want
  10. Try your luck and have fun!!!
